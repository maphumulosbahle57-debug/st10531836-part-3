NAME: BALE STUDIOS PTY LTD 
ORGANISATION DESCRIPTION: 
BALE studios is a fashion brand that aims to sell timeless and ethical fashion clothing. The 
business aims to ensure that the quality of their clothing pieces is high, visually pleasing, and 
comfortable.  
Alot of people find it hard to find clothes that are minimal, does not have harsh branding, high 
quality, comfortable and keeps you classy at the same time. That is why BALE studios was created 
in order for everyone to feel and look exquisite. BALE studios will provide “old money” clothing that 
has minimal branding and gives off a bold statement and immaculate aura to the person wearing 
the clothes. 
BALE studios is for the people who want to look classy without putting in much effort. 
REASONS FOR CHOOSING THE ORGANISATION: 
The reason for choosing a clothing brand is because I noticed a gap in the fashion world (Abbat, 
Centobilli et al, 2023). Alot of clothing stores either have minimal timeless- limited edition pieces, 
harsh branding or low-quality fabric. That would eventually lead one to resorting to custom-made 
clothing, which can be time consuming. Others continually buy clothing which is a waste and 
possibly damage the environment soon because of the constant use of environmental resources 
with a lack of regeneration of these resources (Lu, 2025). BALE studios therefore prevent issues like 
these by providing high-quality clothing that lasts longer, timeless pieces of clothing that can 
withstand any fashion trend, minimal branding and colors.  
WEBSITE GOALS AND OBJECTIVES: 
• The website's goal is to have a fully functioning and 99.99% accurate digital changing room that 
will be able to style and match items from our clothing store, as well as have an almost precise 
sizing for customers. 
• The website also needs to be globally recognized for it to benefit the business while also being 
competitive because of its unique features. 
• Keep the website updates with new information or fashion updates related to the business. 
• Ensure that customers have a positive UX.

PART 2 POE 
Feedback from Part 1 
From Part 1, I added a new file to my website called CSS. The use of this file was to elevate the 
website and give it more life and class. I used things such as; font-family, font-size, letter spacing 
etc. This new file was able to make the website look better in every way and it elevated its 
elegance by making the brand as luxurious as the website is. The use of items-align was also 
incorporated to ensure that the header and footer section of the website was still visible and 
easily accessible as the user scrolls down or up. This made navigation easier and will enhance 
the UX.  
Additionally, colors were also incorporated to fit the elegance of the brand.  A neutral and warm 
tone was used for the header and footer section. A drop-down list was also used for the wish list 
with all clothing items added on the wish list allowing the user to select whichever item they want 
in their wish list.  
Lastly, the Home page was also changed to make the website appear more elegant as the brand 
name. An image was added to be the face of the brand and the logo was placed on the middle of 
the picture using items-align. 
PART 3 POE 
For Part 3, the whole website was elevated. The colors shifted from a warm tone to a more 
elegant and contrasted color which was the use of black and cool yellow gold. I used constants 
and other applicable variables to make the website look more luxurious. A search bar was added 
for men's clothing, kids' special clothing, and women's clothing. A new page was also added 
which was for enquiries, and it also navigates freely as the other pages. The pictures look nicer 
and smaller.  
All the pages are linked together; they all work perfectly, and they show the user if they have 
entered something incorrect or if there's any missing information. The website works well and it 
is a true representation of the brand and will 99.99% give the user a great experience.
